About Us

PINOY TRAVELS

Initially created to serve Filipino expatriates in the UAE with their travel and visa needs. As we grow, we remain dedicated to offering exceptional service to our expanding global customer base.

OUR MISSION

To create unique and socially responsible travel experiences, inspiring people through innovative methods, and ensuring high satisfaction, making us their first choice for travel.

OUR VISION

To be the best provider of professional and innovative travel experiences for the next generations of the traveling society.

Pinoy Travels and Tourism is dedicated to providing the highest quality of customer service to all of our customers globally.

We strive for Excellence in providing the quality of service that will exceed the expectations of our customers.

We commit to meeting the needs of individuals, stakeholders, and groups by maximizing our resources and exercising great Responsibility.

Through Loyalty, we promote professionalism and respect for diversity, ensuring our partners and clients remain loyal to our brand and services, making us their trusted travel companion.
